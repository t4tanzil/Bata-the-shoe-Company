About Us
Comfort is the Future
BATA

Bata Group is a family-owned company that has been providing customers around the world with the best shoes at the best prices, backed by unparalleled service, for over 125 years. We live the Bata way by improving the lives of every community in which we operate. This means supporting our customers, teams and the community itself, as well as providing opportunities for personal and professional growth.Founded in 1894, Bata designs stylish, comfortable footwear at surprisingly affordable prices. We sell over 150 million pairs of shoes a year in our 5,300 retail outlets and produce locally in our 21 Bata-owned manufacturing plants on five continents.
